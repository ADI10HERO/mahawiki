Batman and Joker are fighting again. To fight Joker better Batman has brought a Lazer-Gun called Knight.
This gun can target and destroy any entity in it's range,  which is same as a Knight in a chess game. 
The guns will also destroy self guns when found in range. Given 'n' and 'm' as dimensions of a  n x m ChessBoard,
Find the number of Knights that can be arranged such that the Knights do not harm each other .

Constraints - 

T = 1000
1<=N,M<=10^9

Sample Example :- 
n = 3 , m = 3 

* _ *
_ * _
* _ *

Where " * " denotes the position of the Knights .

OUTPUT = 5
